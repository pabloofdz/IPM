The "mural of the tunas" that raises controversy in Burela (Galicia)

Critics do not understand that tuna have been chosen to decorate a central space of what is considered the Galician capital of the bonito del Norte that is Burela. «Diferénciase ben un atún dun bonito. O bonito ten a aleta máis longa e a cabeza tamén máis alongada. Hai diferenzas», say retired sailors, who say that it is tuna that decorates the wall and not specimens of Thunnus alalunga. There is talk of "confusion", but they see it relatively serious in a seaport.
